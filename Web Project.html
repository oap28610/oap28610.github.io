Dolphin Home Page

This webpage is all about dolphins! Dolphins are aquatic mammals and live in several different bodies of water. They can be found in saltwaters, like the ocean, or in freshwaters, like rivers. My favorite type of Dolphin are Atlantic Spotted Dolphins because they are the ones I grew up seeing at the beach. Find out a little about all the different types adn choose your favorite!
